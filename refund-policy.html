Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

Cataseek is a digital subscription service (e-commerce search and on-demand hosting) operated by Gofenice. Because every plan starts with a free trial, you can fully evaluate the service before paying. This policy explains how cancellations and refunds work.

💡 Try before you buy: every new account gets a 14-day free trial — no payment details required. We encourage you to test Cataseek thoroughly during the trial.

1. Cancelling Your Subscription

  • You can cancel any time from your dashboard: Billing → Cancel Subscription (or Hosting → Cancel Hosting for hosting plans). No emails, no phone calls needed.
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you keep full access to everything you've paid for until then.
  • No further charges are made after cancellation.

2. Refunds

First-time subscriptions — 7-day money-back guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your first paid subscription, contact us within 7 days of the first charge and we will refund the full amount. This applies once per customer.

Renewal payments

  • Renewal charges are generally not refundable, since you can cancel before any renewal from your dashboard.
  • If you forgot to cancel and have not used the service after the renewal, contact us within 7 days of the charge — we review such cases sympathetically and may issue a full or partial refund at our discretion.

Duplicate or erroneous charges

Duplicate payments, charges after a confirmed cancellation, or amounts charged in error are always refunded in full. Notify us with the payment reference and we'll fix it.

Service failure

If a verified, prolonged outage on our side prevented you from using the service for a significant part of a billing period, we will offer a pro-rated refund or account credit.

3. How to Request a Refund

  • Email info@gofenice.com from your registered account email.
  • Include your store name and the invoice number or Razorpay payment ID (both are shown in Billing → Billing History).
  • We respond to refund requests within 2 business days.

4. Refund Processing Time

  • Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method via Razorpay within 5–7 business days of approval.
  • Depending on your bank or card issuer, it can take an additional 5–10 business days for the amount to reflect in your account.

5. Hosting Plans

On-demand hosting plans follow the same rules: cancel any time effective at period end, 7-day money-back on the first hosting purchase, duplicate/erroneous charges always refunded.

6. Contact

Questions about billing, cancellations, or refunds: info@gofenice.com — or see our Contact page.
