Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gofenice ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use the Cataseek platform ("Services"). It applies to merchants who create Cataseek accounts and to visitors of this website.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Store name, store domain, email address, and a hashed password when you register.
  • Billing information related to your subscription (plan, invoices, payment status). Card/UPI details are collected and processed directly by Razorpay — they never touch our servers.

Product & usage data

  • Product catalogue data you sync to us (names, descriptions, prices, images, attributes) so we can index and search it.
  • Search queries made through your storefront widget, used to provide the service and to generate analytics for you.
  • Technical logs (API request counts, timestamps, error logs) for security, rate limiting, and billing.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the search and hosting Services.
  • To process subscription payments and issue invoices (via Razorpay).
  • To send transactional emails — welcome emails, invoices, renewal and trial-expiry notices.
  • To enforce plan limits, detect abuse, and keep the platform secure.
  • To comply with legal obligations (tax, accounting, lawful requests).

We do not sell personal data, and we do not use your product catalogue for anything other than serving your own store's search.

3. Payment Processing

Payments are handled by Razorpay Software Private Limited, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. When you pay, you provide payment credentials directly to Razorpay under their privacy policy. We receive only payment status, a payment reference ID, and the billing details necessary to issue your invoice.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

The dashboard uses browser local storage to keep you signed in (an authentication token). This website uses no third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only with service providers necessary to run Cataseek:

  • Razorpay — payment processing;
  • infrastructure providers that host our servers and databases;
  • email delivery providers for transactional emails.

Each provider is bound to process data only on our instructions. We may disclose information if required by law.

6. Data Retention

  • Account and billing records are kept for as long as your account exists and thereafter as required by tax and accounting law.
  • Product data and search analytics are deleted when you delete your account (or on written request).
  • Expired-trial accounts may be removed after a reasonable dormancy period following prior notice.

7. Security

Passwords and API secrets are stored hashed (bcrypt). Traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). Access to production systems is restricted. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data using industry-standard measures.

8. Your Rights

You may access, correct, export, or delete your personal information at any time — most of it directly from your dashboard (My Account), or by emailing info@gofenice.com. We respond to verified requests within 30 days.

9. Children

The Services are intended for businesses and are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced by email or in-app notice before they take effect.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: info@gofenice.com — or see our Contact page.
